SmashON is a user-friendly mobile app designed for badminton enthusiasts. You can easily reserve courts, chat with other players using Firebase as database, and authenticate via Gmail. The app further enhances security and convenience with a QR code entrance pass system, allowing users to gain access to the facility in a contactless manner. Developed with Flutter framework, SmashON provides a seamless experience for Android users, making it a go-to solution for hassle-free court reservations and community interaction.
